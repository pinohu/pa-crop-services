# PHASE 1: LEGAL FOUNDATION
**Time:** 45 minutes active work + mailing time
**Cost:** $125 (LLC) + $70 (CROP filing) + $6 (certified mail) = $201
**Prerequisites:** A computer, internet access, a checking account, and your Erie PA street address.

## 1.1 Get an EIN (Employer Identification Number) [HUMAN REQUIRED]
- **What:** A tax ID number for your LLC from the IRS. Free. Required for everything else.
- **Go to:** https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
- **Click:** "Apply Online Now"
- **Click:** "Begin Application"
- **Select:** "Limited Liability Company (LLC)"
- **Select:** "Started a new business" as reason
- **Select:** "Pennsylvania" as state
- **Enter:** 1 for number of members (or however many members your LLC will have)
- **Select:** "Banking purposes" when asked why you need an EIN
- **Enter your info:** Your full legal name, SSN, address
- **Entity name:** PA Registered Office Services, LLC
- **Responsible party:** Your full legal name
- **Click:** Submit
- **SAVE THE EIN NUMBER.** Screenshot it. Write it down. You need it for everything that follows.
- **Download the EIN confirmation letter (CP 575).** Save as PDF.
- **Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cost:** $0

## 1.2 Form the Pennsylvania LLC [HUMAN REQUIRED]
- **What:** Create PA Registered Office Services, LLC as a legal entity with the PA Department of State.
- **Go to:** https://file.dos.pa.gov
- **If you don't have a Keystone Login account:**
  - Click "Register" to create one
  - Fill in your name, email, create password
  - Verify your email
  - Log back in
- **Once logged in:**
  - Click the "Initial Forms" tab
  - Click "Certificate of Organization — Domestic LLC" (Form DSCB:15-8821)
  - **Field: LLC Name:** PA Registered Office Services, LLC
  - **Field: Registered Office:** Select Option A (your own address)
  - **Enter your Erie street address** (the physical address where you will receive mail as a CROP)
  - **County of venue:** Erie
  - **Organizer name:** Your full legal name
  - **Effective date:** "Upon filing" (default)
  - The system will prompt you to complete the **Docketing Statement (DSCB:15-134A)** — fill in all required fields
  - **Pay:** $125 by credit/debit card
  - **Submit**
  - The system processes online filings automatically — you'll get confirmation within minutes
  - **SAVE YOUR ENTITY NUMBER** from the confirmation. Screenshot it. You need this for everything.
- **Output:** Certificate of Organization, Entity Number
- **Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cost:** $125

## 1.3 Call the Bureau of Corporations [HUMAN REQUIRED]
- **What:** Confirm the exact CROP registration procedure before mailing anything.
- **Call:** (717) 787-1057 (or toll-free: (888) 659-9962)
- **Say exactly this:** "Hi, I just formed a Pennsylvania LLC called PA Registered Office Services, LLC, entity number [YOUR NUMBER]. I want to register as a Commercial Registered Office Provider under 15 Pa.C.S. § 109. I have a cover letter and statement of address prepared. Is there a specific form I should use, or will these documents suffice? What is the exact filing fee?"
- **Write down everything they tell you.** If they have a specific form, use that instead of the enclosed documents.
- **Ask:** "How long does it take to appear on the published CROP list?"
- **Ask:** "Can I email or fax the documents instead of mailing?"
- **Time:** 5-10 minutes
- **Cost:** $0

- **Fill in ALL blank fields on both documents:**
  - Your full name
  - Your Erie street address (the CROP office address)
  - Your phone number (the business number — see Phase 3)
  - Your email (hello@pacropservices.com — set up in Phase 3)
  - Entity name: PA Registered Office Services, LLC
  - Entity number: [from step 1.2]
  - Today's date
- **SIGN both documents** in the signature lines
- **Print both documents**
- **Write a check:**
  - Amount: $70.00 (or whatever the Bureau told you in step 1.3)
  - Payable to: "Commonwealth of Pennsylvania"
  - Memo: "CROP Registration — PA Registered Office Services, LLC"
- **Put in an envelope:**
  - Cover Letter (signed)
  - Statement of Address (signed)
  - Check
- **Address the envelope to:**
  ```

  Pennsylvania Department of State
  P.O. Box 8722
  Harrisburg, PA 17105-8722
  ```
- **Return address:** Your Erie PA address
- **Send via USPS Certified Mail with Return Receipt** (at the post office, costs ~$6)
- **Save the certified mail tracking number**
- **Time:** 20 minutes
- **Cost:** $70 (check) + ~$6 (certified mail) = $76

## 6.4 Build the Annual Report Reminder Workflow [AI CAN DO]
- **What:** The core value proposition. Daily check for clients approaching their annual report deadline.
- **In n8n:** Create new workflow "CROP — Annual Report Reminders"
- **Node 1: Schedule Trigger**
  - Cron expression: `0 9 * * *` (runs daily at 9 AM)
- **Node 2: Get All Active Clients**
  - HTTP Request to SuiteDash API → GET /contacts (filter by role=client)
  - OR: HTTP Request to AiTable → GET records from clients table
- **Node 3: Calculate Days Until Deadline (Code node)**
  ```javascript
  const today = new Date();
  const year = today.getFullYear();

  const deadlines = {
    'Domestic Business Corporation': `${year}-06-30`,
    'Foreign Business Corporation': `${year}-06-30`,
    'Domestic Nonprofit Corporation': `${year}-06-30`,
    'Foreign Nonprofit Corporation': `${year}-06-30`,
    'Domestic LLC': `${year}-09-30`,
    'Foreign LLC': `${year}-09-30`,
    'Domestic LP': `${year}-12-31`,
    'Foreign LP': `${year}-12-31`,
    'Domestic LLP': `${year}-12-31`,
    'Business Trust': `${year}-12-31`,
    'Professional Association': `${year}-12-31`,
  };

  const results = [];
  for (const client of $input.all()) {
    const entityType = client.json.entity_type || 'Domestic LLC';
    const deadline = new Date(deadlines[entityType] || `${year}-12-31`);
    const daysUntil = Math.ceil((deadline - today) / (1000 * 60 * 60 * 24));

    if ([90, 60, 30, 14, 7].includes(daysUntil)) {
      results.push({
        json: {
          ...client.json,
          days_until_deadline: daysUntil,
          deadline_date: deadline.toISOString().split('T')[0],
          reminder_type: `ar-${daysUntil}-day`
        }
      });
    }
  }
  return results;
  ```
- **Node 4: Switch (route by days remaining)**
  - 90 days → Send 90-day reminder email
  - 60 days → Send 60-day reminder email
  - 30 days → Send 30-day reminder email (more urgent)
  - 14 days → Send 14-day warning email
  - 7 days → Send 7-day final warning email
- **Node 5-9: Email nodes** (one per reminder level, using templates from Operations Bible)
- **Node 10: Log to AiTable** (record that reminder was sent)
