# Google Business Profile — Setup Guide
## PA CROP Services

### Why This Matters
When someone searches "PA registered agent near me" or "Pennsylvania CROP Erie PA", 
Google shows a map pack of local businesses BEFORE organic results. Without a GBP 
listing, PA CROP Services is invisible to these searches.
