Bethlehem's Business Landscape

Bethlehem's economic transformation from heavy industry to a mixed economy has produced a diverse set of business entity types. The hospitality and entertainment sector around Wind Creek Bethlehem includes multiple LLC structures for gaming, hotel, and food service operations. Lehigh University and Moravian University generate a steady flow of faculty-founded startups, technology transfer entities, and research-adjacent LLCs. St. Luke's University Health Network, one of the largest employers in the region, anchors a network of medical practice entities across Northampton and Lehigh counties.

Bethlehem straddles Lehigh and Northampton counties. This dual-county situation does not affect PA registered office requirements — PA entity registration is entirely state-level — but it does mean that litigation involving Bethlehem businesses may be filed in either Lehigh County (Court of Common Pleas, 455 W Hamilton St, Allentown) or Northampton County (Court of Common Pleas, 669 Washington St, Easton), depending on where the relevant events occurred.
