Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZenBusiness a licensed PA CROP?+
No. ZenBusiness provides registered agent services in Pennsylvania as part of their formation and compliance bundles, but they do not hold a PA CROP license under 15 Pa. C.S. \u00a7 109.
How does ZenBusiness pricing compare to PA CROP Services?+
ZenBusiness registered agent service costs $149/year standalone or is included in their Pro ($199/year) and Premium ($349/year) bundles which also include formation services. PA CROP Services starts at $99/year for registered office only, with hosting and annual report filing included at higher tiers.
I formed my LLC through ZenBusiness. Can I switch my registered agent to PA CROP Services?+
Yes. File DSCB:15-108 with the PA DOS ($5 fee) to change your registered office address. You can keep your ZenBusiness formation and switch only the registered agent portion to PA CROP Services.
Does ZenBusiness handle PA-specific compliance?+
ZenBusiness offers compliance reminders across all 50 states through their Worry-Free Compliance product. PA CROP Services focuses exclusively on Pennsylvania and provides PA-specific guidance including 2027 dissolution deadline management.
