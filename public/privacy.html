Privacy Policy

Effective: March 22, 2026 | Last updated: March 22, 2026

1. Who We Are

PA Registered Office Services, LLC ("PA CROP Services," "we," "us") is a Commercial Registered Office Provider licensed in Pennsylvania under 15 Pa. C.S. § 109. Our address is 924 W 23rd St, Erie, PA 16502.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Entity information: business name, entity type, PA DOS file number, registered office history
  • Payment information: processed by Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Communications: emails, chatbot conversations, support requests

Information collected automatically:

  • Website analytics via Plausible Analytics (privacy-focused, no cookies, no personal data)
  • Session recordings via Microsoft Clarity (anonymized interaction data for UX improvement)
  • Portal login timestamps and feature usage for engagement scoring

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide registered office and compliance services as described in our Terms of Service
  • To send transactional emails: welcome messages, document notifications, annual report reminders, payment receipts
  • To send marketing emails: compliance updates, service announcements (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To provide AI-powered compliance assistance through our chatbot
  • To calculate client health scores for proactive service improvement
  • To process referral commissions for our partner program

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate PA CROP Services:

  • Stripe (stripe.com): Payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • SuiteDash (suitedash.com): Client relationship management and document storage.
  • Acumbamail (acumbamail.com): Email marketing and newsletter delivery.
  • Emailit (emailit.com): Transactional email delivery (welcome emails, reminders, notifications).
  • Groq (groq.com): AI language model powering our compliance chatbot. Chatbot conversations are processed by Groq's API but are not used to train their models.
  • 20i (20i.com): Web hosting for clients on hosting-included plans.
  • Plausible Analytics (plausible.io): Privacy-focused website analytics. No cookies. No personal data.
  • Microsoft Clarity (clarity.microsoft.com): Anonymized session recordings for UX improvement.
  • Vercel (vercel.com): Website hosting and serverless function execution.

5. Data Retention

We retain client data for the duration of the service relationship plus 3 years for legal and tax compliance purposes. Documents received at your registered office are retained in digital form for 7 years. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@pacropservices.com.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including: HTTPS encryption on all pages, encrypted API communications, access controls on client data, and regular security reviews. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: (a) access your personal data; (b) correct inaccurate data; (c) request deletion of your data; (d) opt out of marketing emails; (e) request a copy of your data in a portable format. To exercise these rights, contact hello@pacropservices.com.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies: (a) Stripe checkout session cookies (essential for payment); (b) portal authentication cookies (essential for login). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. Plausible Analytics operates without cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed at individuals under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy with 30 days' notice via email. The current version is always available at pacropservices.com/privacy.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries: hello@pacropservices.com or 814-228-2822.
