The 2027 Deadline Affects Allentown Businesses Too

Pennsylvania’s 2027 dissolution enforcement deadline applies to all PA registered entities, regardless of where in the state they operate. Allentown businesses that have not filed any annual reports by December 31, 2027 face administrative dissolution — and foreign entities cannot reinstate. Venue County Note

Pennsylvania law allows courts to serve legal process at your registered office address. Our registered office is located in Erie County. For most Pennsylvania business matters, venue is determined by where the business operates — but if your contract or operating agreement designates venue county, verify it reflects your preference.
