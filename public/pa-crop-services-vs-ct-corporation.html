Frequently Asked Questions

Is CT Corporation a licensed PA CROP?+
No. CT Corporation provides registered agent services in Pennsylvania but does not hold a PA CROP license under 15 Pa. C.S. \u00a7 109. They operate under a different framework as a national registered agent provider.
How much does CT Corporation charge for PA registered agent service?+
CT Corporation\u2019s PA registered agent pricing starts around $320\u2013$400 per year depending on the engagement. Enterprise clients with multi-state portfolios may negotiate volume pricing. PA CROP Services starts at $99/year.
When does CT Corporation make sense over PA CROP Services?+
CT Corporation is a strong choice for enterprise clients managing dozens or hundreds of entities across multiple states who need a single compliance vendor with deep corporate governance expertise. For most PA small businesses with 1\u20135 entities, PA CROP Services may be the right fit.
Can I switch from CT Corporation to PA CROP Services?+
Yes. File DSCB:15-108 with the PA DOS ($5 fee) to update your registered office, then notify CT Corporation per your service agreement. Check your CT contract for any cancellation notice requirements.
