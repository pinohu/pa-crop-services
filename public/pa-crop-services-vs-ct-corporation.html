About CT Corporation

CT Corporation has been operating since 1892 and is now part of the Wolters Kluwer legal and compliance division. Their client base is primarily large corporations, law firms, and compliance departments. They offer comprehensive corporate services including registered agent, UCC filings, corporate formations across all 50 states, trademark monitoring, and their flagship CT Navigator compliance management platform.

CT Corporation’s Pennsylvania service provides a compliant registered office address, document receipt, and forwarding. Their document handling is professional and reliable. For enterprise clients who need PA as one of many states in a national compliance portfolio, CT Corporation delivers a proven service.

Where CT Corporation Has a Clear Advantage

  • Enterprise compliance infrastructure: CT Navigator is a full corporate compliance platform used by Fortune 500 legal departments. If you need integrated UCC filing management, trademark monitoring, and multi-state entity management in one system, CT Corporation’s platform is purpose-built for that.
  • 130+ years of operation: CT Corporation has an established track record that matters to risk-averse corporate clients.
  • Law firm relationships: Many large law firms route client registered agent work through CT Corporation. If your counsel manages your compliance, they may already have a CT account relationship.
  • Multi-state coverage: For businesses with entities in 10, 20, or 50 states, managing them all through one vendor reduces administrative overhead. CT Corporation’s scale across all states is unmatched.

Where PA CROP Services Has a Clear Advantage for PA Businesses

  • Price: $99/year vs $320–$400+/year. For a Pennsylvania-only business, paying for enterprise infrastructure you will never use adds cost without benefit.
  • Licensed PA CROP: PA CROP Services holds a PA CROP license under 15 Pa. C.S. § 109 (DOS #0015295203). CT Corporation does not hold a PA CROP license under this specific framework. See why the CROP license matters.
  • Annual report filing included: Our Business Pro plan ($349/year) includes PA annual report filing. At CT Corporation, annual report filing is a separate engagement with additional fees. For a PA LLC paying CT Corporation $350/year for registered office plus $150–$200 for annual report filing, PA CROP Services’ Business Pro at $349 — which includes both — is the better value.
  • PA 2027 deadline specificity: CT Corporation sends generic multi-state compliance alerts. PA CROP Services sends reminders tuned specifically to your entity type and the PA annual report deadline (LLC = September 30, corporation = June 30, LP/other = December 31). This matters when the 2027 enforcement window is the single biggest compliance risk facing PA businesses.
  • Real physical PA address: Our service address is 924 W 23rd St, Erie, PA 16502 — a real commercial office. Not a national sorting facility. Not a third-party mail handler. Same-day scanning and same-day notification are standard.

The PA 2027 Deadline Factor

Pennsylvania’s Act 122 of 2022 created a new annual report requirement effective January 1, 2025. Starting in 2027, entities that miss their annual report deadline face administrative dissolution (domestic entities) or permanent termination with no reinstatement (foreign entities). This is a PA-specific enforcement mechanism that has no national equivalent at this scale.

For a national provider like CT Corporation, Pennsylvania is one of 50 states. Their compliance calendar is built for multi-state clients. The PA-specific urgency of the 2027 deadline — particularly the entity-type distinction (LLC vs corporation vs LP deadlines differ) — is not something their system is built to track with the granularity a PA specialist can provide.

PA CROP Services’ entire compliance operation is built around Pennsylvania. When September 30 approaches for LLC clients, or June 30 for corporation clients, that is our primary operational focus. Read the 2027 dissolution deadline analysis.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CT Corporation a licensed PA CROP?▼
CT Corporation provides registered agent services in Pennsylvania but does not hold a PA CROP license under 15 Pa. C.S. § 109. They operate as a national registered agent provider. PA CROP Services holds a PA CROP license (DOS #0015295203).
How much does CT Corporation charge for PA registered agent service?▼
CT Corporation’s PA registered agent pricing starts around $320–$400 per year depending on the engagement and service tier. Enterprise clients with multi-state portfolios may negotiate volume pricing. PA CROP Services starts at $99/year.
When does CT Corporation make sense over PA CROP Services?▼
CT Corporation is a strong choice for enterprise clients managing dozens or hundreds of entities across multiple states who need a single compliance vendor with deep corporate governance expertise. For most PA small businesses with 1–5 entities, PA CROP Services offers more relevant expertise at a much lower price.
Can I switch from CT Corporation to PA CROP Services?▼
Yes. File DSCB:15-108 with the PA DOS ($5 fee) to update your registered office, then notify CT Corporation per your service agreement. Check your CT contract for any cancellation notice requirements. The switch takes 7–10 business days to process at the PA DOS.
Does PA CROP Services handle PA annual report filing like CT Corporation does?▼
Yes. PA CROP Services includes annual report filing on Business Pro ($349/year) and Business Empire ($699/year) plans. CT Corporation offers annual report filing as a separate add-on. PA CROP Services’ pricing includes filing so there are no surprise add-on fees.
What is CT Navigator and does PA CROP Services have an equivalent?▼
CT Navigator is CT Corporation’s enterprise compliance management platform for large multi-entity clients. PA CROP Services offers a compliance portal included on all plans, purpose-built for PA small businesses managing 1–10 entities. For sole PA focus, our portal provides what you need without the enterprise complexity and cost.
