Our Mission

Pennsylvania's 2025 annual report requirement and 2027 dissolution deadline are creating a compliance challenge that most PA business owners don't know about yet. With 3.8 million registered entities and only ~65 licensed CROPs in the entire state, there's a massive gap between the compliance infrastructure businesses need and what's available.

PA CROP Services exists to fill that gap. We provide local, professional compliance infrastructure — same-day document scanning, 5-tier deadline alerts, a real compliance dashboard, and direct access to a team that knows Pennsylvania's rules — at a price that works for every PA business, from solo LLCs to multi-entity family offices.

Our registered office at 924 W 23rd St, Erie, PA 16502 is a real commercial address. Documents are physically received, scanned same-day, and uploaded to your portal. If you are served with legal process, we will have it to you that day.
