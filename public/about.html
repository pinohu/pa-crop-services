About PA CROP Services

Our Founder

Ikechukwu P.N. Ohu, PhD founded PA CROP Services to bring rigorous, credential-backed compliance infrastructure to Pennsylvania businesses of every size — from solo LLCs to multi-entity family offices.

Dr. Ohu is a Full Professor and Program Director of Industrial & Robotics Engineering at Gannon University in Erie, Pennsylvania. He holds a PhD in Engineering Science with a Human Factors specialization — a discipline that is fundamentally about designing systems that work reliably for real human beings. That same precision applied to engineering systems drives how PA CROP Services is designed: every notification, every document, every deadline is handled with documented process and backup protocol.

He is a Pennsylvania-licensed Notary Public, an IRS Enrolled Agent (in progress), a licensed real estate professional, and a mortgage loan originator. He has secured nearly $1 million in research funding and published 100+ scholarly works. The same rigor that generates published research is applied to your compliance records.

Our Mission

Pennsylvania's 2025 annual report requirement and 2027 dissolution deadline are creating a compliance crisis that most PA business owners don't know they're in. 3.8 million registered entities. Only ~65 licensed CROPs in the entire state. Most businesses are using national registered agents who have never read 15 Pa. C.S. § 109 and don't understand what makes Pennsylvania's system categorically different.

PA CROP Services exists to fix that. We are the local, credentialed, precisely-run alternative to impersonal national RAs — at a price that makes professional compliance infrastructure accessible to every PA business, not just large corporations.

Our registered office at 924 W 23rd St, Erie, PA 16502 is a real commercial address. Documents are physically received, scanned same-day, and uploaded to your portal. If you are served with legal process, we will have it to you that day — not lost in a national forwarding queue.
