About Incfile / Tailor Brands

Incfile was acquired by Tailor Brands in 2022 and now operates as part of a broader business formation and branding platform. Their strength is in helping entrepreneurs form LLCs and corporations quickly and affordably. They handle formation filings in all 50 states and bundle registered agent service into their formation packages.

Their registered agent service is straightforward: they provide a state address for registered agent purposes in each state where you need one, receive and forward documents, and maintain an online portal for document access. For a newly formed PA business needing the basics, Incfile’s first-year bundle is a reasonable starting point.

PA CROP Services charges $99/year from the start. For businesses that formed through Incfile and are approaching their renewal date:

The 2027 Deadline and Why Formation Providers Often Miss It

Incfile’s core business is formation — helping you create a new entity. Annual report compliance is a downstream service they have added to their portfolio. When Pennsylvania introduced Act 122 of 2022 with its new annual report requirements (effective 2025, enforcement 2027), providers with a formation-first mindset were slower to develop PA-specific compliance workflows than providers whose entire business model is built around ongoing PA compliance.

The 2027 enforcement deadline creates a binary outcome: entities either file by their deadline, or they face administrative dissolution (domestic entities) or permanent termination (foreign entities). A provider who sends you a generic “annual report season is coming” email is fundamentally different from one who knows your LLC’s September 30 deadline and sends reminders at 90, 60, 30, 14, and 7 days before it. The Verdict

Incfile is a legitimate, functional registered agent service and an excellent formation platform for new businesses. The free first year is a genuine value for new PA LLCs. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Incfile a licensed PA CROP?▼
No. Incfile (now part of Tailor Brands) provides registered agent services in Pennsylvania but does not hold a PA CROP license under 15 Pa. C.S. § 109. PA CROP Services holds a PA CROP license (DOS #0015295203).
Is Incfile’s free registered agent service really free?▼
Incfile offers a free first year of registered agent service when you form your business through them. After the first year, the service renews at $119/year. PA CROP Services charges $99/year from the start with no introductory pricing that later increases.
Can I switch from Incfile to PA CROP Services after my free year?▼
Yes. File DSCB:15-108 with the PA DOS ($5 fee) to change your registered office address to 924 W 23rd St, Erie, PA 16502. Cancel your Incfile registered agent renewal before it charges. The PA DOS processes changes in 7–10 business days.
Does Incfile handle PA annual reports?▼
Incfile (Tailor Brands) offers annual report filing as a paid add-on service. PA CROP Services includes annual report filing on Business Pro ($349) and Business Empire ($699) plans at no additional cost.
I formed my LLC through Incfile. Can I switch just the registered agent to PA CROP Services?▼
Yes. Your formation through Incfile is separate from your registered agent arrangement. You can keep your formation history, EIN, and operating documents while switching only the registered office to PA CROP Services. The change is made through the PA DOS, not through Incfile.
How does PA CROP Services compare to Incfile on Pennsylvania 2027 deadline compliance?▼
Incfile’s compliance reminders are built for all 50 states and are not tuned to Pennsylvania’s specific entity-type deadlines (LLC = September 30, corporation = June 30, LP/other = December 31). PA CROP Services provides PA-specific reminders timed to your exact entity type, with particular focus on the 2027 enforcement window.
