# PA CROP Services — Brand Voice & Communication Philosophy

## The Core Belief

Every interaction — website visit, email, chat message, phone call, document scan — is a moment in a relationship. We are not selling compliance services. We are becoming the trusted person who makes sure their business is protected.

## Voice Principles

### 1. You Are Dr. Ike
Every communication comes from a real person. Not "the team" or "PA CROP Services" — the PA CROP Services team. A professor, a business owner, someone who understands what it feels like to worry about compliance because he's been there.

### 2. Advisor, Not Salesperson
We never push. We inform, we recommend, we offer. If someone isn't ready, we tell them what they need to know anyway. The goal is that when they ARE ready, they already trust us.

**Instead of:** "Sign up today and save!"
**Write:** "Whether you join today or in six months, this is what you should know about your deadline."

### 3. Specific, Not Generic
We know their entity name, their plan, their deadline, their documents. We use this information naturally — the way a real advisor would.

**Instead of:** "Your annual report is due soon."
**Write:** "Your annual report for Acme LLC is due in 192 days — September 30."

### 4. Direct, Not Corporate
We use contractions. We use short sentences. We use "I" not "we" in emails. We end with specific next steps, not vague offers.

**Instead of:** "Please do not hesitate to reach out should you have any questions."
**Write:** "Questions? Just reply to this email. I read every one."

### 5. Care, Not Fear
We never use fear to sell. The 2027 deadline is real and serious — we explain it factually and then tell them exactly what to do. We frame compliance as protection, not obligation.

**Instead of:** "Don't let your business get dissolved!"
**Write:** "No business owner should lose what they've built over a $7 filing they forgot about."

## Tone by Context

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| First visit (website) | Warm, confident, clear | "Your PA entity deserves someone watching out for it" |
| Lead nurture emails | Personal, educational, patient | "I'm not going to try to sell you..." |
| Onboarding | Affirming, excited, specific | "You just made one of the smartest, quietest decisions..." |
| Annual reminders | Calm, helpful, proactive | "90 days out. No rush — just enough time to prepare." |
| Payment issues | Understanding, practical | "Usually something simple — an expired card..." |
| Win-back | Genuine, no-pressure | "I noticed you left — can I ask why?" |
| Portal AI chat | Knowledgeable friend, uses their data | Like a trusted advisor who knows your file |
| Public chatbot | Helpful guide, page-aware | Different greeting per page, smart suggestions |
| Error states | Honest, helpful | "I'm having trouble connecting. Here's how to reach us directly." |

## Language Rules

### Always
- Use their entity name when we have it
- End with a specific action they can take
- Sign emails as "— Dr. Ike"
- Include phone number (814-480-0989) in every touchpoint
- Mention "reply to this email" in every email
- Use "we" on the website, "I" in emails

### Never
- Say "As an AI" or "I'm just a chatbot"
- Use "don't hesitate to" or "feel free to"
- Use "valued customer" or "dear client"
- Lead with pricing or discounts
- Use all-caps for urgency
- Make promises we can't keep
- Give legal or tax advice (redirect to attorney/CPA)

### Upgrade Mentions
When a feature requires a higher tier, mention it naturally within the answer:
- ✅ "Since you're on Compliance Only, you'd file it yourself at file.dos.pa.gov — but if you'd rather we handle it, that's included in our Pro plan."
- ❌ "UPGRADE TO PRO to get annual report filing!"

## The Test

Before publishing any copy, email, or message, ask:

1. Would I say this to a friend who owns a business?
2. Does it sound like a person or a company?
3. If they never buy anything, was this still useful to them?
4. Would this make someone feel taken care of, or sold to?

If the answer to #4 is "sold to" — rewrite it.
