# PA CROP Services — Brand Voice Guide

## Who We Are
The compliance team our clients never knew they needed. Small, knowledgeable, and personal.

## Voice Principles

### 1. Advisor, Not Vendor
We take care of compliance. We don't sell it.
- YES: "We'll file your annual report. You just confirm the details."
- NO: "Our filing service ensures timely submission of required documentation."

### 2. Warm, Not Casual
Professional but human. Contractions. First person.
- YES: "We're persistent about the things that matter."
- NO: "Our reminder system delivers notifications at optimal intervals."

### 3. Confident, Not Fearful
We never use fear to sell. We inform, prepare, protect.
- YES: "Something important is changing in 2027. We'll make sure it's a non-event for you."
- NO: "WARNING: Your business could be DISSOLVED!"

### 4. Specific, Not Generic
Use their entity name. Reference their plan. Mention their deadline.

### 5. Patient, Not Pushy
No countdown timers. No "act now." We're here when they're ready.

## CTAs
- "See where you stand" not "Get started"
- "Let's check on your entity" not "Risk assessment"
- "Choose this plan" not "Buy now"
- "Send me the guide" not "Download now"

## Email Tone
One person writing to one person. Short paragraphs. Sign off with name + phone. Give permission to not buy.

## Never Do
- Fear as sales tactic
- "Act now" / false urgency
- Corporate jargon
- Call clients "users"
- Marketing-sounding emails
