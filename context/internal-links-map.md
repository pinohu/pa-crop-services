## Linking Rules

1. **Minimum 3 internal links per blog post, 5 per pillar guide**
2. **Every article links to `/compliance-check` at least once** (this is the primary lead capture)
3. **Every article links to the homepage pricing section at least once**
4. **City pages link to 2-3 nearby city pages** (once city pages exist)
5. **Never use "click here" or "read more" as anchor text**
6. **Vary anchor text** — don't use the same phrase twice in one article
7. **Place at least 2 internal links in the body of the article** (not just header/footer)
8. **First internal link should appear within the first 300 words**
